How To Play

Rock paper scissors is a classic luck based game in which each player selects one of the three items. The items are then compared using these rules:
Rock > Scissors, Scissors > Paper, Paper > Rock

Select either rock, paper, or scissors. The computer will also select one. Your selection will be compared with the computer's and the program will show if you have won, lost, or tied.
